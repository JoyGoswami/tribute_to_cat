A Tribute to my cat

The name of my cat is Meenu. My mother used to give food to stray cats. Meenu is one of the stray kittens who came to our house. The little kitten was a playful, joyous kitten. Though he was a male baby we named him Meenu. But one day the kitten came to my house injured and backbone broken with blood stains on his back. We all thought the kitten would become paralyzed but he rose and walked again. He is two years old now. But he is currently suffering from Pneumonia surviving on antibiotics. Please keep my Meenu in your prayers.
